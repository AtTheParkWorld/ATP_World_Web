11 · Cancellation Policy
11 / 12
Fair to both sides · ATP always keeps 10% of any coach earnings

If something comes up.

Both members and coaches can cancel — and both can explain the reason. The system handles refunds automatically. ATP always retains 10% of whatever the coach earns from any session.

SCENARIO MEMBER GETS YOU GET (NET)
Member cancels 24+ hours before 100% to wallet — (no service)
Member cancels 2-24 hours before 50% to wallet 45% (50% − 10%)
Member no-shows · or cancels under 2h 0% 90% (100% − 10%)
YOU cancel · any time 100% to wallet — (no service)

📝 Explain the reason

Both coach and member can add a short note when cancelling. The other party sees it. Helps keep cancellations respectful and accountable.

⚠️ Three strikes

Three no-shows or last-minute cancellations within 12 months triggers a 30-day platform suspension for either side. Quality matters.
