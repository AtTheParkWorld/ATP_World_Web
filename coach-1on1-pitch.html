08 · The Money
8 / 10
No hidden fees. No surprises.

How the money moves.

A single Stripe transaction handles everything. ATP never holds your money — it goes straight from member to bank.

AED 250
Member pays
→
AED 225
To you · 90%
AED 25
To ATP · 10%

No platform fees on you

The 10% is the only deduction. There's no monthly subscription, no listing fees, no withdrawal fees.

Stripe handles the rest

Card processing fees (~3%) are paid by the member, not you. We use Stripe Connect — the same system used by Uber and Airbnb.

Paid weekly

Every 7 days your earnings auto-deposit to your bank. No invoicing. No requesting payment.

09 · Cancellation Policy
9 / 10
Fair to both sides

If something comes up.

Members and coaches both deserve protection. Here's exactly how cancellations and no-shows work — automatic, no arguments.

WHEN THE MEMBER CANCELS MEMBER GETS YOU GET
24+ hours before the session 100% 0%
2 to 24 hours before 50% 50%
Under 2 hours · or no-show 0% 100%
If YOU cancel · any time before 100% + 10% credit 0%

No-show repeated three times in a year triggers a 30-day suspension on the platform. We protect quality on both sides.
