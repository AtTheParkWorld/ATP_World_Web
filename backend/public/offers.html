FAQ

Quick answers

Do I need to be a member?
Yes. Member offers are part of the perks of being in the ATP community. Joining is free — sign up takes 30 seconds and you'll have a points balance immediately.
How do I earn points?
Every session you attend banks points. Joining challenges, hitting streaks, referring friends, posting in the community feed — all top up your balance. Your profile shows the exact breakdown.
How long is my discount code valid?
Most codes are good for 90 days from the moment you generate them. Some partner offers have shorter or longer windows — check the offer card before you redeem.
Can I get my points back if I don't use the code?
Points are deducted the moment you generate a code, but if the partner confirms you never used it we'll refund. Email general@atthepark.world with the code and we'll sort it out.
How do race / event tickets work?
For paid events (marathons, races, clinics), the partner runs their own checkout. Tap the offer and we'll send you straight to their booking page — usually with a discount applied for ATP members.
Are you a brand? How do I list an offer here?
We work with brands that align with the ATP community — fitness, wellness, recovery, nutrition, events. Pitch us here →
