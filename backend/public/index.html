Our story

Why we started
At The Park

It started with a simple sentence.
"Let's meet at the park after work."

No website. No logo. No plan. Just Fredy and Tatiana — two people who believed that movement shouldn't cost a thing, and that the hardest part of fitness isn't the workout. It's doing it alone.

What began at Al Formal Park in Abu Dhabi in 2015 with five colleagues is now the UAE's largest free outdoor fitness community — 7,000+ registered members across Dubai, Al Ain and Muscat.

"We didn't build a community. We just kept showing up. And so did everyone else."

Back in 2015, Fredy and Tatiana arrived in Abu Dhabi from London, building teams and opening stores for Nike. They were surrounded by people passionate about sport — people who believed in what they were selling. But many had never truly experienced it themselves.

Something kept bothering them.

How can you talk about movement… how can you inspire someone to be active… if you've never really lived it?

So one afternoon, they sat on the grass with a few colleagues. No equipment. No pressure. Just simple bodyweight exercises and honest conversation. No memberships. No expectations. Just people showing up.

What happened next

At first, it was small. A few friends. A few colleagues. And honestly — they had no idea what it would become. But they remember how people started to feel after those sessions. Smiling more. Stronger. More confident. But what stayed with them most was something quieter:

They didn't feel alone anymore.
That's when they realized… this was never just about fitness.

People brought their friends. Then their families. Even customers joined. And without trying to build anything — a community built itself.

Today, At The Park runs free fitness sessions in Dubai, Al Ain and Muscat every week. Running, yoga, crosstraining, kickboxing, cycling, dance and more. Different levels. Different stories. But all of them with one thing in common:

They showed up.
Why it's always free

Most people don't struggle with fitness because they're lazy. They struggle because they're doing it alone. People start strong, life gets busy, motivation drops. Not because they don't care. But because it's hard to keep going on your own.

That's why At The Park exists. Not to replace gyms. Not to compete with anyone. But to give people a place where they feel comfortable starting — and even more importantly, continuing.

Everything we do is free. Fitness should be accessible to everyone. It shouldn't depend on money, background, or experience. You shouldn't have to think twice before deciding to take care of yourself.

Maybe you're reading this
thinking about starting again.

Or maybe you've tried before and stopped. Or maybe you've always felt like fitness just isn't for you. We get it. We've seen that story so many times. That's why we're telling you this personally — you don't need to be fit to start. You don't need to be ready. You just need to show up.

If you ever feel like you're doing this alone… you don't have to.
