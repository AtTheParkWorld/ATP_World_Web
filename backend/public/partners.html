Common questions

How quickly do you respond?

Within 48 hours, on business days. Most inquiries get a same-day reply because the founder personally looks at every one.

Can we tailor a custom package?

Yes — tiers are starting points. If your brand has specific activation goals (product launches, recruitment days, content shoots), we'll build a custom package around them. Title Sponsor tiers are particularly bespoke.

What's the typical commitment length?

Most partnerships run on 12-month agreements with quarterly performance reviews. We do offer 3-month pilot programs for new brands — gets you measurable impact data before going long-term.

Are sessions still free for members if you take partners?

Yes — absolutely non-negotiable. Free is the foundation. Partner contributions cover coaching, equipment, gear, and growing the community. Members never pay.

Do we get exclusivity in our category?

Champion and Title Sponsor tiers include category exclusivity (e.g. only one beverage partner, one apparel partner). Community Partners share the page with up to 12 other brands across non-competing categories.

How do you measure success?

Quarterly impact reports: reach, engagement, member sentiment (we survey), session attendance, social mentions, and any partner-defined KPIs (foot traffic, sample redemptions, sign-ups, etc.).
