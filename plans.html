Common questions

Is Free for Life really free?

Yes — forever. No card, no trial that expires, no hidden fees. Free members can book any session, earn points, and join the community. Premium is optional for members who want extra perks.

Can I cancel Premium anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your profile — you keep Premium access until the end of the billing period, then you roll back to Free. No cancellation fee, no questions asked.

What's the difference between Premium and Premium Plus?

Premium gets you the premium-only sessions and 2× points on every check-in. Premium Plus adds a monthly 1-on-1 coach call, exclusive member events, and partner perks across the city (gyms, cafés, recovery centres).

Do I need to be Premium to book a session?

No. Every member — Free or Premium — can book every regular session. Premium just unlocks the premium-only sessions on top.

How do referrals interact with plans?

Every member, free or paid, gets a personal referral code. Share it — you earn points every time someone uses it. Premium members earn a 200-point bonus when their referrals upgrade to Premium themselves.
